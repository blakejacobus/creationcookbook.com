# Chocolate Peanut Butter Protein Smoothie

## Source

Mt. Zion High School football training

## Ingredients

| Serving | Ingredient | Notes |
|-|-|-|
| 1 scoop | ice | |
| 3/4 cup | milk | |
| 1/4 cup | peanut butter | |
| 1/4 cup | PB powder | |
| 1/4 cup | cocoa powder | |
| 1 scoop | protein powder | |
| 1 | banana | |
| To taste | chocolate syrup | Add to taste |

## Instructions

1. Add the ice, milk, peanut butter, PB powder, cocoa powder, protein powder, banana, and chocolate syrup to a blender.
1. Blend until smooth and creamy.
1. Adjust the milk and ice to make the smoothie thinner or thicker depending on your preferred consistency.
