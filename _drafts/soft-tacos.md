# Soft Tacos

## Source

Soft tacos
    - Your cooking instructions for the beef 
    - Optional refried or black beans
    - Tomatoes, avocado, black olives, lettuce, cheese, sour cream 

## Ingredients

| Serving | Ingredient | Notes |
|-|-|-|
| 2 tbsp | Olive oil |  |
| 2-4 tbsp | Taco seasoning | Amount varies based on preference | 
| 1 bag | Gardein Ground Be'f | Can substitute other ground beef/alternative proteins |
| 1 can | Black beans | Can substitute refried |
| 1 can | Black olives |  |
| 2-4 cups | Lettuce | Shredded |
| 1-2 whole | Tomatoes | Diced |
| 1-2 whole | Avocados | Sliced or scooped |
| 1 bag | Cheese | Shredded; mexican mix preffered |
| 1 tub | Sour cream |  |
| Various | Tortillas | Based on serving size |

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in frying pan over low/medium heat.
1. Add desired amount of ground beef and top with taco seasoning.
1. Mix meat in pan and add cheese once taco seasoning is mixed in; remove from heat.
    - Amount of cheese is based on amount of meat; consider 1 part cheese to 3 parts meat to start.
1. Remove meat/cheese mixture and place in bowl.
1. Add black beans to pan and cook until warm; remove from heat; add to another bowl.
1. Prepare veggies and toppings as desired; consider placing on one or more cutting boards or additional bowls to make it easier to serve.
1. Steam tortillas.
1. Organized ingredients and mix into tortillas to make tacos. Serve.
