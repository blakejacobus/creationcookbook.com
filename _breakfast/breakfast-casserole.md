# Breakfast Casserole

## Ingredients

| Serving | Ingredient | Notes |
|-|-|-|
| 1 lb | Plant-based sausage |  |
| 1 tsp | Mustard powder |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Salt |  |
| 5 whole | Eggs | Beaten |
| 2 cups | Milk |  |
| 6 slices | Bread | Toasted and cubed |
| 8 oz | Shredded cheese | Mild, cheddar |
| 1 cup | Spinach | Chopped |
| 1-2 whole | Bell peppers | Diced |

## Instructions

1. Cook sausage according to instructions; let cool.
1. Combine eggs, milk, and seasonings.
1. In a mixing bowl, add vegetables, bread, sausage, cheese, and stir together.
1. Pour mixture into greased 9x13" baking dish.
1. Cover and chill in overnight.
1. Bake at 350 degrees:
    - 45-60 minutes covered
    - Additional 30 minutes uncovered
