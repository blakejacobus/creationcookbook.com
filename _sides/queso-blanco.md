# Queso Blanco

## Ingredients

| Serving | Ingredient | Notes |
|-|-|-|
| 1 1/2 tbsp | Butter |  |
| 4 oz | Cream cheese | Softened |
| 3 slices | Pepper Jack |
| 3–5 tbsp | Milk | Add more for thinner consistency |
| 1 tsp | Chili powder |  |
| To taste | Garlic salt |  |

## Instructions

1. In a saucepan over medium-low heat, melt the butter, cream cheese and sliced Pepper Jack, stirring frequently.
2. Add 3 tablespoons of milk, chili powder, and garlic salt; continue to stir until the mixture is fully melted and smooth.
3. Add more milk (1 tablespoon at a time) until the queso reaches your desired consistency.
4. Serve warm with tortilla chips or as a topping for nachos.
