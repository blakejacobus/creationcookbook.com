# Veggie Burgers & Fries

## Ingredients

| Serving | Ingredient | Notes |
|-|-|-|
| 4 | MorningStar Spicy Black Bean Burgers |  |
| 2 | Brioche buns |  |
| 1 | Bag of frozen french fries | Recommendation: Alexia |
| 2 | Cheese slices | Pepperjack or gouda |
| 1 | Avocado |  |
| 1 tsp | Honey mustard |  |

## Instructions

1. Cook fries in the oven or air fryer according to packaging instructions.
1. Cook burgers on a skillet or in a pan.
1. Add butter to inside of buns and cook until golden brown on skillet/pan.
1. Slice avocado and spread on bottom bun.
1. Add patty and condiment(s).
1. Serve with fries.
