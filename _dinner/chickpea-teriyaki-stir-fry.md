# Chickpea Teriyaki Stir Fry

## Ingredients

| Serving | Ingredient | Notes |
|-|-|-|
| 1 can | Chickpeas |  |
| 1 whole | Squash  |  |
| 1 whole | Zucchini |  |
| 2 whole | Bell pepper |  |
| 2 pouches | Black Rice / Quinoa | 90 second mix |

## Instructions

1. Chop vegetables.
1. Rinse and drain the beans.
1. Cook vegetables and beans at 450F for 20 minutes.
1. Cook rice in the microwave per packaging instructions.
1. In a large bowl, mix everything with teriyaki sauce.
